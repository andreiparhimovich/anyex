Create surveys with ease, no technical expertise required.

How to use:
1
Add Questions
Start by adding your desired questions to the survey. You can choose from some question types such as multiple-choice, single-choice, free-text, or rating questions.
2
Adjust Settings

Provide a concise and informative description to set the context for your survey.

Set an expiration date for your survey to control the duration during which participants can submit their responses.

Protect your survey results by enabling result protection with a pin code.

3
Get Links

Once you're satisfied with your survey, generate unique links to share with your audience. Use these links in emails, social media, or embed them on your website. Collect responses conveniently and effortlessly.

4
Real-time Results

Once you've created a survey and started collecting responses, you can effortlessly view the results in real-time on a dedicated page.
